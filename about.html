about me

My name is Nikoloz Macharashvili. I am a United States Citizen. Been in front end development for past 2.3 years.

I look at front development like a way of life rather than a job. My belief is that developing delightful user interfaces is tied to who I am as a person and what my purpose is in life. Like Yashimoto Musashi said "the way(mastery) is in practice", so I practice everyday, even on weekends.
